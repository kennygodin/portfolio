The title Gratitude Journal

What I am greatful for

A gratitude journal is a diary of things for which someone is grateful. Keeping a gratitude journal is a popular practice in the field of positive psychology. The app was build with HTML/CSS, Javascript, Node.js using the CRUD operations of MongoDB to create, remove, update and delete items from the database.
